How to Use This Page (Quick TD Guide)

1. Build or load your bracket. Use the Brackets tab or AYPT to seed entrants.

2. Set up the room. Use Room Setup to drag tables into place.

3. Assign matches. Click Assign Table on a match, then choose an open table from Links.

4. Generate Link. Creates a scoreboard URL, QR code, Match Sheet link, and Local AHMS handoff.

5. Keep flow moving. Mark tables Playing, Open, Assigned, or Offline as the tournament room changes.
